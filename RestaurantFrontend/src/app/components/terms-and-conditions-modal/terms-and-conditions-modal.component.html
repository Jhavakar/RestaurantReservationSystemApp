Terms and Conditions

Please read these terms and conditions carefully before making a reservation.

  • Cancellation Policy: Cancel at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a fee.
  • No-Show Policy: A fee of $20 per person will be charged for no-shows.
  • Credit Card Hold: We may require a credit card hold for reservations.
  • Arrival Time: We hold your table for 15 minutes past your reservation time.
  • Party Size Changes: Notify us of any changes to your party size at least 2 hours before your reservation.
  • Dining Duration: Please note that there is a 2-hour dining limit during peak times.
